ABOUT ME

  I’m Mariel Gonzalez! I’m in CALS at Cornell University, originally from NYC. I love being creative in everything I do, whether it’s school, projects, or just messing around with new ideas. Music is my biggest obsession—I’m always listening to something, making playlists, or deep-diving into my favorite artists. Basically, if it involves creativity and being adventurous, I’m all in!

I created this business to be more than just a place to get your nails done—I wanted it to be a space where anyone can feel safe to sit, talk, be silent, or just take a break from everything. College life can be hectic, and I know how important it is to have affordable self-care, so I made sure my services are accessible to everyone. Getting your nails done should be fun, creative, and stress-free. Nothing makes my day more than seeing someone’s whole vibe change after a fresh set—it’s all about feeling good, confident, and happy!

Who we are

Variety of Designs 

We offer a variety of nail painting services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From simple manicures to intricate nail art designs, our talented team is equipped to bring your nail dreams to life. We use high-quality, non-toxic polishes and tools to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.  

Affordable prices 

Our services are affordable, making it easy for students and community members to indulge in a little pampering without breaking the bank. Additionally, we host themed nail painting events and workshops, allowing participants to learn new techniques and explore their creativity in a supportive environment. 

student run 

Supporting our organization means investing in the personal growth and well-being of students in our community. By choosing our nail services, you contribute to a student-led initiative that helps us sustain our operations, allowing us to provide affordable services and provide for our clients.  

join the community  

We invite you to be a part of our journey by supporting our nail painting organization. Whether you’re looking for a fun way to express your style, a unique activity to enjoy with friends, or an opportunity to contribute to a meaningful cause, we have something for you.
